How to Play

  • Use the arrow keys to move left and right.
  • Press the Up-Button to jump.
  • Jump on enemies to defeat them.
  • Collect bottles for throwing them.
  • Press the Hand-Button to throw a bottle to damage enemies.
  • Collect coins to increase your score.
  • Defeat the end boss to win the game.

    • How to Play

  • Press the 'S' key to start the game.
  • Use the arrow keys to move left and right.
  • Press the spacebar to jump.
  • Jump on enemies to defeat them.
  • Collect bottles for throwing them.
  • Press the 'D' key to throw a bottle to damage enemies.
  • Collect coins to increase your score.
  • Defeat the end boss to win the game.
  • Press the 'P' key to pause the game.
  • Press the 'ESC' key to leave and go back to start screen.
